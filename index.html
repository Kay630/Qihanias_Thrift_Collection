About

Welcome to Qihania's Thrift Collection! We are a fashion-forward, eco-conscious brand offering a wide selection of high quality secondhand fashion.

At Qihania's Thrift Collection, we believe in more than just clothes. We believe in creating a community where fashion is not only stylish but also sustainable. Our carefully curated collection includes everything from classic staples to one-of-a-kind treasures, all sourced with care and love for the environment.
